Visual Identity - Embody Presence

Embody Presence was created to help explore and share true healing though learning to see clearly, beyond the distortion of mental filters. These filters create the illusion of separation from our natural and abundant source of wellbeing. It is a brand to communicate a direct, intuitive and experiential understanding of that source of wellbeing. The lotus represents the purity we all have already inside of us, it just needs to be uncovered. This project was made for a client in the UK.
