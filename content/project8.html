Implementing a rebrand - Oral Unic

Oral Unic is a major dental office franchise in Brazil. During its rebranding, I was the lead designer responsible for implementing the new visual identity and communication style at the franchiser's office. I also had the opportunity to create new visual assets based on the updated brand guidelines. Additionally, I contributed to the design of the new website. Please note that the language are in Portuguese, as this rebranding project took place in Brazil.
