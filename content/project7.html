Rebranding project - O padeiro

O Padeiro was a renowned artisan bakery in southern Brazil. As the brand evolved, it needed a rebrand to refresh its visual identity and bring a more modern look to the company. I contributed by designing a new logo and creating a communication guide for its use. Please note that the language are in Portuguese, as this rebranding project took place in Brazil.
